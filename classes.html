Early Years

Welcome to Reception, where your child begins their exciting school journey. In our Early Years classroom, children learn through play, exploration, and meaningful hands-on experiences. We focus on developing early reading, writing, and number skills, while also nurturing confidence, independence, and social development. Our safe and welcoming environment encourages curiosity, creativity, and a love of learning that will carry pupils through their primary school years.

Teachers in this class:

  • Class Teacher: Mrs Emily Carter
  • Teaching Assistant(s): Miss Chloe Andrews, Mrs Priya Shah

Year 1

Welcome to Year 1, an important and exciting step as children transition from play-based learning into more structured activities. Pupils continue to build their phonics knowledge, develop early reading fluency, and strengthen their understanding of number and simple problem-solving. Lessons remain practical, engaging, and full of creativity, helping children adapt smoothly while fostering enthusiasm and confidence in their learning.

Teachers in this class:

  • Class Teacher: Miss Megan Hill
  • Teaching Assistant(s): Mrs Louise Parker

Year 2

Welcome to Year 2, where children become increasingly confident, responsible, and independent learners. This year, pupils deepen their reading comprehension, write with more detail and accuracy, and build secure mathematical skills. They enjoy rich topic work across the curriculum, developing knowledge of the world around them. Year 2 is also the final stage of Key Stage 1, and children take part in end-of-KS1 assessments in a calm, supportive, and encouraging environment.

Teachers in this class:

  • Class Teacher: Mr Billy Cliff
  • Teaching Assistant(s): Miss Katie Howard(Mon, Tues, Wed)

Year 3

Welcome to Year 3 - the beginning of Key Stage 2. Children take on new routines and greater independence as they move into the juniors. Pupils extend their skills in reading, writing, and maths, explore exciting new curriculum topics, and learn to work collaboratively in groups. Year 3 offers many opportunities for creativity, problem-solving, and personal growth as children become more responsible and resilient learners.

Teachers in this class:

  • Class Teacher: Mrs Evelyn King
  • Teaching Assistant(s): Miss Hannah Reid

Year 4

Welcome to Year 4, a year filled with exploration, confidence-building, and teamwork. Children continue developing strong foundations in literacy and maths, deepen their understanding of science, and explore fascinating historical and geographical topics. Pupils also work hard to secure their times tables ahead of the national Multiplication Tables Check. With increased maturity and enthusiasm, Year 4 learners embrace challenges and grow both academically and socially.

Teachers in this class:

  • Class Teacher: Mr Adam Patel
  • Teaching Assistant(s): Might draw on support from other classrooms

Year 5

Welcome to Year 5, where pupils take on more responsibility, develop independence, and begin thinking more critically and creatively. This year features exciting opportunities across the curriculum: engaging texts, challenging problem-solving, hands-on science, and memorable topic work. Pupils also act as role models across the school, demonstrating leadership, teamwork, and resilience as they prepare for their final year of primary school.

Teachers in this class:

  • Class Teacher: Miss Laura Bennett
  • Teaching Assistant(s): Mr Oliver Grant

Pupils work hard to consolidate their learning and prepare for SATs, supported by teachers every step of the way.
