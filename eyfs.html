⭐ What We Learn

  • Early phonics foundations: Children begin recognising letter sounds, blending simple CVC words, and developing confidence in early reading and writing.
  • Number skills through play: Pupils explore counting, patterns, shapes, comparing amounts, and problem-solving using hands-on resources.
  • Exploring the natural world: Children investigate plants, weather, animals, and seasonal change through outdoor learning and sensory play.
  • Developing independence: Building social skills such as sharing, turn-taking, expressing feelings, and forming friendships.
  • Early writing developments: Using mark-making tools, tracing letters, writing names, and beginning simple words and captions.

⭐ Our Classroom

  • Continuous provision areas: Role-play corner, construction area, mark-making table, small world play and creative art station.
  • Outdoor learning environment: Sand pit, water play, climbing equipment, ride-on toys, mud kitchen and nature exploration zone.
  • Sensory and quiet zones: Spaces for calming activities, mindfulness, soft furnishings and cosy reading corners.

⭐ How we Learn

  • Play-based exploration: Hands-on activities encourage curiosity, imagination and problem-solving.
  • Adult-led focus time: Small-group sessions for phonics, early maths and fine-motor activities.
  • Talk-rich learning: Children learn through stories, singing, role-play and language development activities.

⭐ Term 1 - Highlights

  • Settling into school routines and making new friendships.
  • Beginning phonics with Phase 1 and Phase 2 sounds.
  • Learning about ourselves, our families and what makes us unique.

⭐ Term 2 - Highlights

  • Exploring seasonal change through autumn walks and sensory activities.
  • Practising speaking and performing during the EYFS Nativity.
  • Investigating shapes, patterns and early measurement in maths.
