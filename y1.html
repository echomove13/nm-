⭐ What We Learn

  • Phonics Phase 5 mastery: Children learn alternative spellings, tricky words and begin reading with increasing fluency.
  • Early writing structure: Forming sentences with correct punctuation, using simple connectives and expanding vocabulary.
  • Mathematical foundations: Counting in 2s, 5s, 10s, number bonds, simple addition/subtraction, and comparing numbers.
  • Science discovery: Counting in 2s, 5s, 10s, number bonds, simple addition/subtraction, and comparing numbers.

⭐ Our Classroom

  • Inviting learning zones: Writing station, maths resources, reading nook and imaginative play areas adapted for KS1.
  • Reading-friendly environment: A cosy book corner that supports early reading habits.
  • Practical tools: A cosy book corner that supports early reading habits.

⭐ How we Learn

  • Small-group guided reading and writing: Teacher support tailored to each child’s reading level.
  • Hands-on maths: Using physical objects to model problems and deepen understanding.
  • Exploratory science: Observations, simple tests and practical learning that builds curiosity.

⭐ Term 1 - Highlights

  • Writing simple sentences using capital letters and full stops.
  • Counting forwards and backwards, learning number bonds to 10.
  • Topic focus: “All About Me” with self-portraits and family timelines.

⭐ Term 2 - Highlights

  • Reading longer books with improved fluency and expression.
  • Beginning to skip count in 2s, 5s and 10s.
  • Topic focus: Celebrations Around the World with crafts and research.
