⭐ What We Learn

  • Spelling patterns and grammar: Homophones, common suffixes, expanded noun phrases and punctuation.
  • Developing writing: Story writing, character descriptions, information texts and organising ideas clearly.
  • Maths fluency: Working confidently with 2-digit numbers, simple multiplication/division and understanding fractions.
  • Science investigations: Healthy living, habitats, plants and everyday materials.

⭐ Our Classroom

  • Independence-focused layout: Spaces for choosing resources and completing tasks independently.
  • Visual learning displays: Vocabulary walls, grammar prompts and maths examples.
  • Resourced group tables: Materials for teamwork, problem-solving and structured discussions.

⭐ How we Learn

  • Modelled and shared writing: Materials for teamwork, problem-solving and structured discussions.
  • Using manipulatives: Base-10 blocks, counters and cubes for clearer maths understanding.
  • Investigation-based science: Children predict, test and draw conclusions from practical experiments.

⭐ Term 1 - Highlights

  • Developing joined-up handwriting and using expanded noun phrases.
  • Learning about plants and their needs through practical investigations.
  • Topic: The Great Fire of London with an exciting history focus.

⭐ Term 2 - Highlights

  • Writing information texts, stories and character descriptions.
  • Using maps and atlases in geography to study the UK.
  • Topic: Festivals & Traditions, including Christmas crafts and performances.
