⭐ What We Learn

  • Handwriting and paragraphing: Children learn joined handwriting and how to organise longer pieces of writing.
  • Developing maths strategies: Column addition/subtraction, times table recall and introduction to fractions.
  • Science extension: Rocks, soils, light, shadows, forces and the human skeleton.
  • Eventful history topics: The Stone Age, Ancient Egypt or local history (depending on school curriculum).

⭐ Our Classroom

  • Independent learning stations: Research shelf, writing table, maths challenge corner and topic areas.
  • Interactive topic displays: Artefacts, vocabulary lists and enquiry questions.
  • Maths resource collection: Number beads, place value charts and times table mats.

⭐ How we Learn

  • Enquiry-led projects: Children investigate questions and present findings.
  • Group collaboration: Practical tasks and discussions that develop reasoning.
  • Reading comprehension practice: Developing inference, prediction and retrieval skills.

⭐ Term 1 - Highlights

  • Learning about rocks, fossils and soils through hands-on exploration.
  • Beginning formal methods for addition and subtraction.
  • Moving to joined handwriting and writing in paragraphs.

⭐ Term 2 - Highlights

  • Learning about forces and magnets with exciting challenges and tests.
  • Writing adventure stories and non-chronological reports.
  • Topic: World Geography, including continents, oceans and climate zones.
