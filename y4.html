⭐ What We Learn

  • Advanced writing skills: Using figurative language, paragraphs, dialogue and report structures.
  • Maths mastery: Times tables to 12×12, two-step problems, perimeter, area and decimals.
  • Science knowledge: Sound, electricity, states of matter and simple circuits.
  • Understanding the world: Geography mapping skills and comparing continents/countries.

⭐ Our Classroom

  • Supportive learning environment: Working walls, sentence starters and problem-solving prompts.
  • Flexible seating: Group tables, floor seating and quiet corners for independent work.
  • Tech-friendly area: Devices for research, typing and digital presentations (if available).

⭐ How we Learn

  • Partner talk and group tasks: Increasing opportunities for peer reasoning.
  • Building independence: Children take more responsibility for organisation and completing work.
  • Practical science: Experimenting with circuits, materials and sound patterns.

⭐ Term 1 - Highlights

  • Exploring local geography including map skills and landmarks.
  • Writing stories, diaries and setting descriptions with expanded detail.
  • Securing column addition and subtraction with 4-digit numbers.

⭐ Term 2 - Highlights

  • Learning about electricity and building simple circuits.
  • Using persuasive language to write letters and adverts.
  • Topic: Europe, exploring countries, capitals and cultural diversity.
