⭐ What We Learn

  • Sophisticated writing: Persuasive texts, balanced arguments, setting descriptions and narrative detail.
  • Upper-KS2 maths: Fractions, percentages, long multiplication, division and volume/area.
  • Science depth: Lifecycles, Earth and space, forces and properties of materials.
  • Geography understanding: Rivers, biomes, climate zones and map skills.

⭐ Our Classroom

  • Resource-rich environment: Writing aids, maths equipment and research materials.
  • Topic displays: Key vocabulary, enquiry questions and pupil projects.
  • Reading culture: A class library with age-appropriate novels and reference books.

⭐ How we Learn

  • Independent projects: Longer assignments and research tasks.
  • Collaborative science experiments: Planning, predicting, recording and evaluating.
  • Debates and discussions: Encouraging critical thinking and confidence.

⭐ Term 1 - Highlights

  • Learning about Earth and Space, including moon phases and planetary movement.
  • Writing balanced arguments and descriptive narratives.
  • Developing confidence with fractions, place value and large numbers.

⭐ Term 2 - Highlights

  • Investigating forces such as gravity, air resistance and friction.
  • Writing biographies and persuasive speeches.
  • Topic: Christmas Around the World with research and presentations.
