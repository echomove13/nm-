⭐ What We Learn

  • Refining writing: Formal letters, reports, narratives, character development and cohesive paragraphs.
  • Maths preparation: Multi-step problems, algebra introduction, ratio, geometry and consolidation of KS2 skills.
  • Science depth: Electricity circuits, evolution, microorganisms and classification.
  • Transition preparation: Study skills, organisation, independence and emotional readiness for secondary school.

⭐ Our Classroom

  • Focused learning zone: Revision guides, anchor charts and challenge tasks.
  • Leadership roles: Prefects, librarians, playground buddies and event helpers.
  • Calm study environment: Quiet spaces for reflection and independent work.

⭐ How we Learn

  • Structured revision sessions: Preparing pupils for assessments with confidence.
  • Team projects: Presentations, research tasks and extended topics.
  • Real-world application: Using maths, writing and science in meaningful contexts.

⭐ Term 1 - Highlights

  • Writing suspense stories, formal letters and independent extended pieces.
  • Tackling multi-step problems in fractions, ratio and algebra basics.
  • Studying World War II — evacuees, rationing and historical sources.

⭐ Term 2 - Highlights

  • Writing newspaper reports, speeches and narrative flashbacks.
  • Strengthening arithmetic fluency before SATs preparation.
  • Investigating electricity and building more complex circuits.
